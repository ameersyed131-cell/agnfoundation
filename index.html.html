Who We Are

A Beacon of Hope,
Rooted in Devotion

The Ajmeri Gharib Nawaz Foundation is a non-profit organization committed to serving the underprivileged, inspired by the sacred teachings of Hazrat Khwaja Moinuddin Hasan Chishti (RA), known to millions as Gharib Nawaz — "Benefactor of the Poor."

We believe that true devotion to the Almighty is expressed through compassionate service to His creation. Our work spans food distribution, education, medical aid, and community welfare — touching lives across Rajasthan and beyond.

Our Leadership

Syed Fazlul Ameen Chishti

Founder & Chairman — Ajmeri Gharib Nawaz Foundation

Syed Fazlul Ameen Chishti is a devoted servant of humanity and a deeply respected figure in the Sufi tradition, hailing from the sacred city of Ajmer Sharif. Inspired by the timeless teachings of Hazrat Khwaja Moinuddin Hasan Chishti (RA), he founded the Ajmeri Gharib Nawaz Foundation with a singular mission — to carry forward the legacy of Gharib Nawaz by serving the poor, the needy, and the marginalized.

Under his visionary leadership, the Foundation has grown into a beacon of compassion, running daily food distribution (Langar), education programs, free medical camps, and welfare initiatives across Rajasthan. For over 35 years, he has personally overseen the Ramzan Sehri & Iftar service at Ajmer Sharif Dargah — feeding hundreds of souls every single day of the holy month.

Syed Fazlul Ameen Chishti embodies the Sufi values of selfless service, unconditional love, and devotion to God through devotion to His creation.

Biography

A Life Devoted to Humanity

Hazrat Khwaja Moinuddin Hasan Chishti (RA), born in 1141 CE, was one of the greatest Sufi saints of the Indian subcontinent. Known lovingly as "Gharib Nawaz" — the Benefactor of the Poor — he dedicated his life to the service of God and His creation.

He settled in Ajmer, India, where he established the Chishti Order. His message of love, peace, and service transcended all boundaries of religion and culture.

His sacred shrine, the Ajmer Sharif Dargah, continues to attract millions of devotees from all faiths every year.

Our Work

Turning compassion into action

01

Food Distribution (Langar)

Following the sacred tradition of Gharib Nawaz (RA), we organize daily food distribution programs to feed the hungry and destitute. Our volunteers serve nutritious meals to thousands every month, open to all — irrespective of religion or caste.

02

Education Support

We provide scholarships, school supplies, tuition support, and mentoring to underprivileged children. Our programs focus on empowering young minds with knowledge and skills.

03

Medical Camps

We organize regular free medical camps providing consultations, medicines, and health screenings in underserved areas.

04

Charity & Relief Work

In times of crisis, we step forward with immediate relief — distributing essentials like food, clothing, blankets, and clean water. Our programs also support orphans, widows, and the elderly.
