About Us

Rooted in faith, driven by compassion

Our Mission

To serve the underprivileged and marginalized communities through food distribution, education support, healthcare, and community welfare programs — inspired by the teachings of Khwaja Gharib Nawaz (RA). We strive to create a world where no one sleeps hungry, no child is denied education, and every person has access to basic medical care.

Our Vision

To build a compassionate society rooted in the Sufi values of love, peace, and selfless service. We envision a world where the light of Gharib Nawaz (RA) reaches every corner — where communities thrive together in harmony, and where the spirit of giving transcends all boundaries.

Our Inspiration

Guided by the Light of Gharib Nawaz (RA)

Hazrat Khwaja Moinuddin Hasan Chishti (RA) — known lovingly as Gharib Nawaz, the Benefactor of the Poor — dedicated his entire life to the service of humanity. His teachings of love, tolerance, and compassion continue to guide millions around the world. Our foundation walks in his blessed footsteps, carrying forward his divine mission of serving all of God's creation without distinction.

Biography

A Life Devoted to Humanity

Hazrat Khwaja Moinuddin Hasan Chishti (RA), born in 1141 CE in Sanjar, Persia, was one of the greatest Sufi saints of the Indian subcontinent. Known lovingly as "Gharib Nawaz" — the Benefactor of the Poor — he dedicated his life to the service of God and His creation.

He traveled extensively, studying under great masters, before settling in Ajmer, India, where he established the Chishti Order in the subcontinent. His message of love, peace, and service transcended all boundaries of religion, caste, and creed.

His sacred shrine, the Ajmer Sharif Dargah, continues to attract millions of devotees from all faiths and walks of life, embodying his eternal message that the doors of divine mercy are open to all.

His Legacy

Teachings of Love & Peace

The teachings of Gharib Nawaz (RA) revolve around three core principles: love for God and His creation, service to the poor and needy, and tolerance towards all people regardless of their background. He emphasized that true spirituality is reflected not in rituals alone, but in how we treat the weakest among us.

Our Work

Turning compassion into action

01

Food Distribution (Langar)

Following the sacred tradition of Gharib Nawaz (RA), we organize daily food distribution programs (langar) to feed the hungry and destitute. Our volunteers prepare and serve nutritious meals to thousands of people every month, ensuring that no one in our community goes to bed hungry. The langar is open to all — irrespective of religion, caste, or social status.

02

Education Support

Education is the key to breaking the cycle of poverty. We provide scholarships, school supplies, tuition support, and mentoring to underprivileged children. Our education programs focus on empowering young minds with knowledge and skills, giving them the tools they need to build a better future for themselves and their families.

03

Medical Camps

Access to healthcare should not be a privilege. We organize regular free medical camps in underserved areas, providing consultations, medicines, and health screenings. Our medical aid programs include eye care camps, dental checkups, and general health awareness drives, bringing quality healthcare to those who need it most.

04

Charity & Relief Work

In times of crisis and calamity, we step forward with immediate relief — distributing essentials like food, clothing, blankets, and clean water. Our charity programs also include supporting orphans, widows, and the elderly with regular financial assistance and emotional support, embodying the spirit of Khidmat.

35 Years of Devotion

About the Initiative

For over 35 consecutive years, the Ajmeri Gharib Nawaz Foundation has been arranging Sehri and Iftar every day during the blessed month of Ramzan at the sacred dargah of Khwaja Gharib Nawaz (R.A).

Every day, between 500 to 800 fasting individuals — devotees, travelers, the poor, the orphaned — are served with love and dignity. No one is turned away. This is the spirit of Gharib Nawaz (R.A) made manifest.

This selfless service is made possible entirely through the generous contributions of well-wishers, donors, and believers like yourself.
